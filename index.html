FAQ
Quick Answers

Do I need prior marketing experience?

No. Our courses are designed for complete beginners as well as working professionals who want to upskill. We have a fast-track stream if you already have 2+ years of experience.

Are classes live or recorded?

All sessions are live with a dedicated mentor. Recordings are available within 6 hours for students who miss a class. You also get a curated library of supplementary resources.

What is the course duration & fees?

Individual specialisations run 6–12 weeks. The flagship Full-Stack Marketer programme is 6 months. Fees range from ₹18,000–₹75,000 depending on the track. EMI and ISA options available.

Is there a placement guarantee?

Yes. Students who complete all assignments and maintain 80%+ attendance receive our 90-Day Placement Guarantee — you study free again if you don't land a role.
